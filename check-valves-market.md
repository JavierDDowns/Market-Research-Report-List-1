<p><p>The check valves market is categorized into several types based on their operational mechanisms. Swing check valves utilize a hinged disc that swings open and closed to allow or prevent flow, ideal for horizontal piping. Lift check valves feature a disc that lifts off its seat to permit fluid flow and provides a more efficient seal, suitable for vertical and horizontal applications. Other check valve types include ball check valves and diaphragm check valves, each designed for specific use cases and fluid dynamics.</p></p>
