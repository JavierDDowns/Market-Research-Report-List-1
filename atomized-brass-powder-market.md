<p><ul><li>Water Atomization</li><li>Gas Atomization</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The atomized brass powder market is primarily segmented into two types: water atomization and gas atomization. Water atomization involves using high-pressure water jets to create fine brass particles, resulting in a uniform size and shape suitable for several applications. In contrast, gas atomization employs inert gas to cool and solidify molten brass, producing powders with controlled morphology and purity. Both methods are widely used in manufacturing processes, including additive manufacturing, where precise material properties are crucial for performance.</p></p>
