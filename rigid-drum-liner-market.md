<p><p>The rigid drum liner market comprises various types based on material: PE (Polyethylene) drum liners are known for their chemical resistance and durability, making them suitable for hazardous substances. PP (Polypropylene) drum liners offer high thermal resistance and strength, ideal for industrial applications. PVC (Polyvinyl Chloride) drum liners are versatile and cost-effective, often used for non-hazardous liquids. PVDC (Polyvinylidene Chloride) drum liners provide exceptional barrier properties, protecting contents from moisture and gases, enhancing product shelf life.</p></p>
