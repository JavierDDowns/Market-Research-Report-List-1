<p><strong>The Automotive Mufflers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Absorptive Mufflers</li><li>Reactive Mufflers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive mufflers market primarily features two types: absorptive mufflers and reactive mufflers. Absorptive mufflers utilize sound-absorbing materials, such as fiberglass, to diminish noise levels by absorbing sound waves. This results in a quieter exhaust sound. Conversely, reactive mufflers employ specific designs, such as resonators and chambers, to manipulate sound waves and cancel out undesirable frequencies through constructive and destructive interference. Both types play crucial roles in enhancing vehicle performance while ensuring compliance with noise regulations.</p></p>
