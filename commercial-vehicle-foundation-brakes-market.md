<p><strong>The Commercial Vehicle Foundation Brakes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>S-Cam Drum Brakes</li><li>Disc Brakes</li><li>Wedge Brakes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Commercial Vehicle Foundation Brakes market encompasses three main types: S-Cam Drum Brakes, Disc Brakes, and Wedge Brakes. S-Cam Drum Brakes utilize a camshaft to push brake shoes against a drum, providing strong stopping power. Disc Brakes offer superior heat dissipation and consistent performance, often preferred for their efficiency and reduced fade. Wedge Brakes employ a wedge mechanism to press brake shoes against a drum, delivering effective braking with fewer components. Each type caters to specific applications and performance requirements in commercial vehicles.</p></p>
