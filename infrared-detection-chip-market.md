<p><ul><li>Uncooled Type</li><li>Cooled Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The infrared detection chip market comprises two primary types: uncooled and cooled infrared detectors. Uncooled types operate at ambient temperatures and use materials like pyroelectric sensors, making them cost-effective and suitable for applications like thermal imaging and security. Cooled types, on the other hand, require refrigeration to enhance sensitivity and accuracy, using technologies like mercury cadmium telluride. They are typically employed in applications requiring high precision, such as military surveillance and scientific research, due to their superior performance in detecting faint infrared signals.</p></p>
