<p><p>The horizontal acid pumps market comprises two primary types: multi-stage pumps and single-stage pumps. Multi-stage pumps feature multiple impellers, allowing them to generate higher pressure levels and efficiently transport corrosive fluids over long distances. In contrast, single-stage pumps are designed for lower-pressure applications, offering a simpler design ideal for applications requiring less head. Both types are crucial in industries handling acids, ensuring safe and effective fluid transfer while maintaining operational efficiency and durability against corrosive substances.</p></p>
