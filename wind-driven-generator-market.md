<p><strong>The Wind-driven Generator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Asynchronous Wind-driven Generator</li><li>Synchronous Wind-driven Generator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wind-driven generator market comprises two main types: asynchronous and synchronous generators. Asynchronous generators, commonly known as induction generators, rely on slip between rotor speed and wind speed, offering robust performance and lower costs, making them popular in variable wind conditions. In contrast, synchronous generators operate at a fixed speed synchronized with grid frequency, providing higher efficiency and better power quality. Both types play crucial roles in harnessing wind energy for electricity generation, catering to diverse operational needs in the renewable energy sector.</p></p>
