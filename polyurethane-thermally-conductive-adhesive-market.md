<p><p>The Polyurethane Thermally Conductive Adhesive market includes two primary types: Thermally Conductive Potting Compounds and Thermally Conductive Gap Fillers. Potting compounds are used to encapsulate and protect electronic components from environmental stress while ensuring heat dissipation. They provide a robust, secure bond while enhancing thermal conductivity. Gap fillers, on the other hand, are utilized to fill voids and gaps between surfaces, improving heat transfer in assemblies. Both types are crucial in applications requiring efficient thermal management and device reliability.</p></p>
