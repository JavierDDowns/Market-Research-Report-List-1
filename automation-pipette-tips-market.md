<p><p>The automation pipette tips market is divided into two main types: non-filtered and filtered pipette tips. Non-filtered pipette tips are primarily designed for standard liquid handling tasks, allowing direct transfer of liquids without special filtration, making them cost-effective for non-contaminated environments. In contrast, filtered pipette tips contain a barrier to prevent aerosol contamination, ensuring that samples remain uncontaminated during transfers. This is crucial for sensitive applications in research and clinical laboratories, where precision and purity are paramount.</p></p>
