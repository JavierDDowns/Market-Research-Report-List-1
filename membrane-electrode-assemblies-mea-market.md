<p><p>Membrane Electrode Assemblies (MEA) are critical components in fuel cells, facilitating electrochemical reactions. MEAs typically come in three types: 3-layer MEA, which consists of a proton-exchange membrane sandwiched between two catalyst layers; 5-layer MEA, which adds gas diffusion layers for improved performance; and 7-layer MEA, featuring additional components for enhanced water management and thermal control. Each type caters to specific operational needs, influencing their adoption in various applications, including automotive, portable power, and stationary energy solutions.</p></p>
