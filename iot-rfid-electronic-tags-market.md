<p><p>The IoT RFID electronic tags market is primarily segmented into passive and active RFID types. Passive RFID tags lack an internal power source and rely on energy from the RFID reader for activation, making them cost-effective and ideal for inventory management. In contrast, active RFID tags are equipped with their own power source, enabling longer range and richer functionalities such as real-time tracking and environmental monitoring. Both types play critical roles in enhancing supply chain efficiency and asset management in various industries.</p></p>
