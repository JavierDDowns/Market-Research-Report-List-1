<p><p>The IoT smart capacitive proximity sensors market is segmented based on ingress protection ratings, which determine the sensors' durability against environmental factors. IP00 sensors offer no protection against solids or liquids, making them suitable for controlled environments. IP01 sensors can withstand vertically falling drops of water, providing limited water resistance. IP02 sensors are designed to handle water droplets falling at a 15-degree angle. IP03 sensors protect against water spray at up to a 60-degree angle. Other classifications include specialized sensors catering to unique applications.</p></p>
