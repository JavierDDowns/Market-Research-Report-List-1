<p><strong>The Industrial Arc Furnaces Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>AC-EAF</li><li>DC-EAF</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Industrial arc furnaces primarily fall into two categories: AC Electric Arc Furnaces (AC-EAF) and DC Electric Arc Furnaces (DC-EAF). AC-EAF uses alternating current to melt scrap metal and is known for its cost-effectiveness and versatility, making it prevalent in steel production. In contrast, DC-EAF utilizes direct current, offering better energy efficiency and higher quality output, particularly for stainless and specialty steels. Both types serve distinct roles in the steelmaking process, catering to varying production needs and material specifications.</p></p>
