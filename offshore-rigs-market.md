<p><p>The offshore rigs market comprises various types used for oil and gas exploration and extraction. Jackups are mobile units that can be towed and deployed in shallow waters, featuring legs that extend to the seabed. Semisubmersibles are buoyant structures anchored to the seafloor, suitable for deeper waters and adverse weather. Drill ships are vessels equipped with drilling apparatus, ideal for deepwater operations due to their mobility. Other types include tenders and barges, each designed for specific operational environments and requirements.</p></p>
