<p><p>The natural gas heavy truck market is primarily divided into two types: LNG (Liquefied Natural Gas) and CNG (Compressed Natural Gas) heavy trucks. LNG heavy trucks are designed for long-haul applications, utilizing cryogenic fuel storage, enabling greater range and efficiency for long distances. In contrast, CNG heavy trucks use compressed natural gas stored at high pressure, making them suitable for regional transport and shorter routes. Both options offer cleaner emissions compared to traditional diesel, supporting a transition to sustainable transportation solutions.</p></p>
