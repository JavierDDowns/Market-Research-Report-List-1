Construction Pumps use magnetic fields to move conductive fluids, suitable for applications in construction and heavy industries. Induction Pumps operate on electromagnetic induction principles, providing efficient fluid movement in industrial settings. Thermoelectric Pumps utilize temperature gradients to drive fluid motion, often found in cooling systems.
